When Foodify's founders Afua, Izzie and Mai started university, they realised how expensive it was to meet up with friends and eat out at great restaurants. They made it their mission to bring the best local restaurants to people's attention while making it possible for everyone to enjoy them with exclusive deals.

By offering amazing deals at over 600 restaurants across the UK that users can find easily on their phones, Foodify has gone from strength to strength. Users can keep up track of the latest deals at their favourite places, discover new restaurants and enjoy trying new cuisines without breaking the bank.

We love food and our goal is to make amazing food and new experiences available to all. That's why each time someone uses Foodify in one of our partner restaurants, a meal is donated to Shelter. Food is not an exclusive right.
